Help / How to Use

1. Enter Total Grams Trimmed

Type the total grams you've trimmed in the input field. This is your main entry point to calculate your bonus pay.

2. Tap 'Calculate' to Get Bonus

Click the green "Calculate" button to instantly see your bonus pay amount based on current NorCal trimming rates.

3. Add Daily Total from Trim Tracker

If you’ve logged your trim work in the Trim Tracker, you can import that total directly into the calculator.

4. Review Your Session History

Each calculation is saved in the session table below. You can edit, delete, or export your history anytime.

5. Export or Clear Data

Use the buttons at the bottom to export your session history as a CSV file or clear all entries if starting fresh.

6. Install the App

You can add NorCal Trim Calculator to your home screen on mobile. Look for 'Add to Home Screen' in your browser menu.
