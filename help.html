Help & How to Use

Overview

The NorCal Trim Tools were created to help cannabis trimmers stay organized, motivated, and accurate with their work. With the Trim Calculator and Trim Tracker, you can easily log your daily trimming weight, calculate bonus pay, and track your progress across bi-weekly pay periods — all directly in your browser with no account or login required.

All of your data is stored locally on your device, and you can export it anytime for backup or reporting. Whether you're tracking a single day or your entire pay cycle, these tools are designed to make your workflow faster, easier, and more transparent.

Using the Trim Calculator

  • Enter the total grams you trimmed for the day.
  • Click "Add Today's Trim Tracker Total" to import your saved grams from the tracker (if logged for today).
  • Click "Calculate" to add the entry to your session board with calculated bonus pay.
  • The session board now groups your entries by bi-weekly pay periods, starting every other Monday.
  • Past pay periods are collapsed by default to keep things clean. Click the [+] to expand and view entries.
  • Use "Export CSV" to save your full session history, or "Clear Current Pay Period" to reset just the active period.

Hourly Pay Calculator

  • Expand the "Hourly & Pay Averages ▼" section under the session board.
  • Enter your hourly rate, total hours worked, overtime hours, and select a date range.
  • Click "Calculate Averages" to see your full pay breakdown: bonus, overtime, total, daily/weekly/biweekly averages.

Using the Trim Tracker

  • Track your grams for each strain using the Trim Tracker page.
  • You can add up to 3 entries per row and click "+" to add more fields.
  • It automatically calculates your daily total and saves it with today’s date.
  • Go back to the calculator and click the green button to import that total.

Tips & FAQ

  • Everything saves automatically: Your data stays in your browser unless manually cleared.
  • Past pay periods are now collapsible: This keeps your view tidy — just expand any previous period to view or edit entries.
  • Date editing is supported: You can click and edit any date or gram total directly in the table. It will automatically move to the correct pay period after saving.
  • Back up often: Use "Export CSV" to keep your data safe before clearing history.

Need More Help?

If something isn’t working, refresh the page or re-check your dates. You can also contact the creator for support.
