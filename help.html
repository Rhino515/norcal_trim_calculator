Help & How to Use

Overview

The NorCal Trim Calculator and Trim Tracker are tools designed to help trimmers track their daily weight and calculate bonus pay. All data is stored locally in your browser.

Using the Trim Calculator

  • Enter the total grams you trimmed for the day in the input box.
  • Click "Add Today's Trim Tracker Total" to automatically import your weight from the Trim Tracker if you've logged it for today.
  • Click "Calculate" to compute your bonus pay based on the current rate.
  • Your result will appear in the session board below, including the bonus amount.
  • Use the "Delete" or "Clear All" buttons to manage your entries.
  • You can also click "Export CSV" to save your records for backup.

Using the Trim Tracker

  • Use this page to log each strain you trim, along with the amount (in grams).
  • You can enter up to three gram totals per row, and click "+" to add more fields as needed.
  • The tracker calculates your daily total automatically and saves it under the current date.
  • Return to the calculator and click the green "Add Today's Trim Tracker Total" button to import your total for bonus calculation.

Tips & FAQ

  • Data is saved locally: Your entries stay saved in your browser unless you clear your cache or use a different device.
  • Language matters: Make sure you use the app consistently in either English or Spanish — mixing them may cause your data to be saved separately.
  • Backing up: Use the Export CSV option on either page to save a copy of your data manually.
  • Date syncing: The calculator only imports tracker data saved under today’s date. If no data is found, double-check that the tracker entry date matches today.

Need More Help?

If something isn’t working, try refreshing the page or re-entering your data. You can also contact the creator directly for support.
